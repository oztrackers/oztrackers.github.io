Community Rules

Respect Others

This is a peaceful and enjoyable community. No harassment or bullying. Generally, be nice and think before you post, to avoid upsetting the group.

No advertising or soliciting

No spam. If you have a service the community might be interested in, Direct Message the admin first.

Stay On-Topic

Respectable general discussion (TCG or otherwise) is great. Keep TCG buying, selling, or trading to that specific group. No controversial discussions, eg politics or religion.


Buy/Sell/Trade Rules

No Scalping

Do not flip recently released retail items at inflated prices. We are collectors and hobbyists, not scalpers. Prices near RRP are OK.

Post Formatting

Consider using [B] for Buying, [S] for Selling, [T] for Trading.

Photo Verification

Include handwritten, timestamped notes for high-value cards.

Do your own research

OzTrackers is not responsible for Whatsapp user verification. Scammers exist, educate yourself and use Paypal G&S or cash at your local police station. All activities are at buyer/sellers own risk.

Condition Accuracy

Honestly describe card/product condition; no counterfeits or proxies allowed.

Private Messaging

Finalize deals and share personal details via private message.

Report

if you see something suspicious, use the report function.
