Buying and Selling TCG on eBay Australia - Best Practices

How to create reliable listings, avoid scams, and use postage and feedback to protect transactions on eBay Australia

Creating strong listings

Use clear titles, high-resolution photos, and full condition descriptions. Include 'graded' or 'ungraded' where applicable and list any imperfections.

Auction vs Buy-it-Now

Auctions can drive competitive bids but require careful reserve planning. Buy-it-Now gives immediate sales at set prices - combine with 'Best Offer' for flexibility.

Feedback and reputation

Check buyer/seller feedback before transacting. High positive feedback and prompt communication reduce risk for both parties.

Handling disputes

Document condition with photos and communicate quickly. Use eBay's resolution tools and keep postage receipts and tracking for evidence.

Postage tips

  • Always offer tracked postage for higher-value items.
  • Consider combined shipping discounts for multiple purchases from the same buyer.
