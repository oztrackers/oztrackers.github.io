How to Price Your Cards for Australian Buyers - Local Market Guide

Practical pricing steps for sellers targeting Australian buyers, covering comps, fees, and shipping adjustments

Research local comparable sales

Search completed listings on local platforms to determine what similar cards recently sold for. Adjust for condition and grading.

Account for fees and postage

Deduct marketplace fees and estimate postage costs to find your net profit. Factor these into the listing price or offer clear combined shipping terms.

Seasonal and regional effects

Demand can change with local events, holidays or new set releases. Price accordingly if there's temporary demand spike.

Final pricing checks

  • List slightly above your desired net to allow for offers.
  • Offer clear returns or 'no returns' policies to match buyer expectations.
