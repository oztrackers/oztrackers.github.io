Putting the starter pack together - quick tips

  • Start by sleeving every card before placing it in a deck box or binder to avoid edge wear.
  • Use toploaders for high-value singles and keep those toploaders in a dedicated binder or display case.
  • Buy a playmat early - it protects cards during games and makes learning more comfortable.
  • Consider one graded card as a display piece to celebrate your collection milestone, but only after researching the grading market and costs.

Where to buy other essentials locally

For Australian buyers, independent game stores, JB Hi-Fi, and specialist online hobby retailers are great alternatives to Amazon if you want local pick-up or to support your community. Marketplaces like eBay Australia are useful for second-hand or discontinued items.
