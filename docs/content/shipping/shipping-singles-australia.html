Shipping Singles Safely in Australia: Packaging, Cost, and Insurance

Packaging checklist, cheapest tracked options, and insurance advice for Australian card sellers

Packaging basics

Use a soft sleeve, top loader or semi-rigid holder, and a rigid mailer to protect corners and edges. For single cards, the standard stack is: penny sleeve, top loader, taped, then placed in a rigid card mailer with cardboard stiffeners.

Postage and tracking

Choose tracked postage for all but the lowest-value sales. Australia Post offers regular tracked services and some private couriers provide competitive rates—compare pricing based on weight and distance.

Insurance and declared value

Declare value and insure shipments for high-value cards. Some platforms offer seller protection only when tracked and insured postage is used.

Packaging suppliers

  • Buy sleeves, top loaders and mailers in bulk to lower per-item cost.
  • Consider combined postage discounts for multiple items sent together.

Tips for smooth transactions

  1. Take clear photos of the packaged card before posting.
  2. Keep receipts and tracking numbers and update the buyer promptly.
  3. Use signature on delivery for very high-value items.
