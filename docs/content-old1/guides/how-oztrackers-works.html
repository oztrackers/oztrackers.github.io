How OzTrackers Works - A Quick Start for Australian Shoppers

OzTrackers helps Australian collectors and shoppers find product restocks and time-limited deals. This short guide explains how our tracker network works, what notification channels are available, and how to get the best results quickly - whether you use email, SMS, or one of our partner Discord communities.

What OzTrackers does

  • Monitors popular Australian retailers and marketplaces for product availability and price changes.
  • Aggregates alerts into easy-to-consume notifications you control - email, SMS, or Discord.
  • Provides curated tracker lists for common categories - TCG, toys, tech, and games.

Notification channels

Email Alerts (free)

Sign up to receive free email alerts for items you follow. Emails include the product name, store, link, and a short verification checklist so you can confirm stock before you buy.

SMS Notifications (premium)

For time-sensitive drops, SMS offers a fast push to your phone. SMS alerts contain a direct, tagged link to the store (where applicable) so you can act quickly.

Partner Discord Communities (premium)

Join partner Discord servers for live chat, community signals, and curated tracker channels. These paid communities often share local tips - like which store in your city tends to restock first.

How tracking works

  1. We watch public product pages and inventory endpoints for changes to stock status and price.
  2. When a change matches your alert criteria (example: "in stock" at JB Hi-Fi), our system queues a notification.
  3. Notifications are verified with a lightweight additional check to reduce false positives.

Example workflow

Follow these steps to maximise your chances:

  1. Create the alert for the specific product or SKU you want.
  2. Choose your notification channel - email for free, SMS for speed, Discord for community backup.
  3. Act fast - open the linked retailer page and confirm availability using the verification checklist in the alert.

Privacy and data usage

We only store the minimal data required to deliver alerts - your email or phone number, the products you follow, and the notification preferences you choose. For more details see our Privacy Policy.

Common FAQ

Do you charge for alerts?

Basic email alerts are free. Premium options like SMS and partner Discord access are paid.

Can I use OzTrackers from outside Australia?

Yes, but our retail coverage and timing are focused on Australian stores and marketplaces, so results will be best for Australia-based shoppers.

Will OzTrackers buy the product for me?

No - OzTrackers only notifies you. You remain responsible for completing purchases at the retailer.
